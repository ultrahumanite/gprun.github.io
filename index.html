RUNNING. CAMARADERIE. COMMUNITY.

About Us

Our small group of runners is made up of all paces and experience levels. We have lifetime experienced runners and people who are brand new to running. Some members want to meet new people, some want to finish a run of 3 miles, some want to train with others, and some want to get a PR. Our team is run by volunteers and our membership is free

Greenpoint Runners Club answers a longing for the camaraderie and motivation of having a team to run with. We welcome and support runners of all abilities.

To join simply show up to one of our scheduled runs. For more information fill out the contact form at the bottom of the page.

Runs

Monday Morning Easy Run
Time: 6:45 AM      Distance: 3-4 miles      Pace: 8-10 minutes per mile      Effort: Comfortable Workout      Type: Easy/Recovery      Meeting Spot: Father Jerzy Popieluszko Square

Come one, come all and start your week off in the finest way possible with the Monday morning run. We are a conversational bunch, hitting 3-4 miles at the roll-over-and-hit-your-snooze-once time of 6:45 AM. Could be a recovery after a weekend of hard running, or just a ‘start the week as you mean to go on’ type thing. We won’t judge.

All paces are welcome—we tend to run in the 9s, but we can speed up or slow down. We did core once, but it didn’t catch on—it’s Monday morning for goodness sake, how virtuous can you be?



Tuesday Morning Recovery Run
Time: 6:30 AM      Distance: 4 miles      Pace: 8-10 minutes per mile      Effort: Comfortable      Type: Fun/Recovery      Meeting Spot: Transmitter Park

Have to get to work early, or do you just like starting your day off while everyone else is deep in REM sleep? Then this one is for you! A fun group of runners from all levels take on the rolling hills from Bed-Stuy to Downtown Brooklyn and beyond in a conversational kinda way.

We usually run around 8:30-9:30 minutes per mile. There’s always a group that hangs back if you haven’t had your coffee yet, and there’s some that go faster (but we don’t judge).



Wednesday Night Road Run
Time: 7:35 PM      Distance: 4-6 miles      Pace: Varies      Effort: Comfortable to Moderate      Type: Moderate      Meeting Spot: Greenpoint Playground

All running paces are welcome. We separate into three pace groups: fast, medium, not so fast. After the run, we meet up at the north side of the McCarren Park track. We will do stretching and a simple core workout followed by any NBR announcements.
